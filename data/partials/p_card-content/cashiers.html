Cashiers Statistics

A scoring system that evaluates the performance of a store by monitoring the activity of it's cashiers.

The score system is built to dynamically accommodate changes as they occur to establish a baseline of the stores overall performance.

Once the baseline is established, the monitoring system compares each individual cashier to the overall store's performance assigning a Level of Concern to the individual cashier as well as the store as a whole.

The Level of Concern is based on a 4 value scale

Cashier is within the stores score.

Cashier is slightly over the stores score.

Cashier is moderately over the stores score.

Cashier is extremely over the stores score.

Cashier has multiple extreme categories.

At anytime, a Level of Concern can change a cashiers indicator color to purple. A purple concern indicates a cashier has one or more subcategories that fall into the extreme. Purple colored indicators should take precedence over all others.
